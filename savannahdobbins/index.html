My relationship to design

My name is Savannah Dobbins! I am a digital photographer studying ux/ui and graphic design at BYU-Idaho. I have seven years of photography experience, and I will be graduating from BYU-Idaho in 2026 with my Bachelor's degree in graphic design with an emphasis in ux/ui and a certificate in frontend development.

Photography is really what got me started in visual storytelling and user-centered digital experiences and I am always exploring new technology and tools that will help me pursue that passion.

I enjoy exploring nature as much as I love exploring the digital world. I can spend hours gardening, looking for cool rocks, or hiking.
