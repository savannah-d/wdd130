About

Savannah Dobbins is a digital photographer and student currently based in Rexburg, Idaho. She has over five years of photography experience. Savannah currently studies graphic design with an emphasis in ux/ui at BYU-Idaho and is working towards her Bachelor's degree. She loves exploring all sorts of technology and tools to help her pursue her passion for visual storytelling and user-focused design.

Savannah's photography focuses on both natural and urban settings. She feels a close connection to the environment that surrounds her and her goal is to capture its true atmosphere.

She has an obsession with light and shadow and finds both to to be incredibly powerful and communicative tools. In her work, she draws on them to bring attention to specific details, to conjure up feelings of peace, wonder, or appreciation in the viewer, and to present the atmosphere of a subject in photographic form.

If you are interested in learning more, please do reach out.
